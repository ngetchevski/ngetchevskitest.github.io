About Me

Nickola Getchevski

Graphic Design, Fine Arts, Game Design; I seldom restrict myself to a single artistic path. Instead, I want my work and life to encompass as many artistic mediums as possible, since I believe that we should all indulge in a little bit of everything so that we don't miss out on unique experiences, while learning what skills to hone. I treat design in the same regard.
